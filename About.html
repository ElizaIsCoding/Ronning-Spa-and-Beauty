Ronning Spa and Beauty - one of the leading sanatoriums and spa complex of Poland, is located in a pine forest near the large national park and water reservoir, in an ecologically clean area of Mazowietski region.

All conditions for year-round treatment and recreation are created here, and an excellent sports and entertainment base helps to spend time not only useful, but also interesting.

New, beautiful buildings, lovingly groomed territory, convenient travel and traditional Polish hospitality guarantee that once you visit us, you will definitely want to come again.

Up to 300 vacationers can undergo treatment at the Ronning Spa and Beauty at the same time. On the territory of the spa complex there are 8 bedroom buildings, from the balconies of which a beautiful view of the pine forest opens.

We provide effective treatment for:

  • cardio-vascular system
  • peripheral nervous system
  • musculoskeletal system
  • pathologies of the musculoskeletal system
  • gynecological diseases
  • urological diseases
